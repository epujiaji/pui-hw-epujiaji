That day started as with all days. You promise to meet and play.

You were going to visit your kingdom again; that was the plan. You both wanted to finish building a big fort you had been planning. You meet in the woods, and they said they just wanted to take a detour and walk around before going to your tree.

But nothing happened, even after hours; nothing happened because your friend did not show up.

You waited and waited, at the tree and back at your houses, and you waited with their mother and your mother too, past sundown and into nightfall..

They simply never came back.

What else is there?
